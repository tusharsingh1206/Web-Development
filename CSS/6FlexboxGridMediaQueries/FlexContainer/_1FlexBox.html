  1. Flexbox is a one-dimensional layout method for arranging items in rows or columns.Items flex(expand) to fill additional space or shrink to fit into smaller spaces.

  2. The items of the flex container will not go outside the box they will manage by himself and adjust them in the box.

  3. Direction of the flex box:

    1. By default Row
    2. Row-reverse
    3. column
    4. column-reverse
